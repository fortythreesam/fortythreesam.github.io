The Money Store - Death Grips (2012)

reviewed by
Sam Drugan

The Money Store - Death Grips
A Album review by Sam Drugan
Copyright 2017 Sam Drugan
RATING (0 TO ****):  1/2

The Money Store turns out to be one of the worst albums of the list. Listening to The Money Store, gave me an intense sense of sadness, not about the lyrics but about the album and what it says about the current state of the music industry . This lifeless album is never about anything other than its noise.

The albums's only saving grace? It's short -- although not nearly short enough. Maybe you'll be lucky, and there'll be a power outage during your listening so you can get your time back.

The Money Store runs 41:08. It is rated R for "strong violence" and would be acceptable for teenagers.
